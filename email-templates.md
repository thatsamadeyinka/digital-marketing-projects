# High Converting Email Templates

Proven email copy that gets opened, read, and clicked.

## Template 1: Welcome Email (Lead Magnet Delivery)

**Subject:** Your [Lead Magnet Name] is ready

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Here's your [Lead Magnet Name]: [Download Link]

Quick question... what's your biggest challenge with [specific problem]?

Reply and let me know. I read every response and might have a solution for you.

Talk soon,
[Your Name]

**Why it works:** Delivers value immediately, starts conversation, builds relationship.

---

## Template 2: Story Based Sales Email

**Subject:** How I [achieved specific result]

**Body:**

[First Name],

Three years ago I was struggling with [specific problem].

I tried everything. Courses, tools, "experts." Nothing worked.

Then I discovered [your solution/method].

Within [timeframe], I [specific result].

Now I help people like you do the same.

If you're tired of [pain point] and want [desired outcome], here's what I recommend:

[CTA Button: Get Started]

[Your Name]

**Why it works:** Relatability, transformation story, clear next step.

---

## Template 3: Urgency Email (Limited Offer)

**Subject:** This ends tomorrow

**Body:**

Quick reminder, [First Name].

[Offer] ends tomorrow at midnight.

After that, the price goes back to [regular price].

If you've been on the fence, now's the time.

[CTA Button: Claim Your Spot]

Questions? Just reply.

[Your Name]

P.S. Seriously, this closes tomorrow. Don't miss it.

**Why it works:** Clear deadline, simple message, no fluff.

---

## Email Sequence Strategy (5 Day Welcome Series)

**Day 1:** Deliver lead magnet + ask question
**Day 2:** Share transformation story
**Day 3:** Provide value (tips, case study, tutorial)
**Day 4:** Soft pitch (introduce offer casually)
**Day 5:** Hard pitch (direct CTA with urgency)

## Subject Line Formulas That Work

- "Quick question about [topic]"
- "This is embarrassing..."
- "[First Name], I have good news"
- "Your [desired outcome] blueprint"
- "Don't open this if [reverse psychology]"

## Metrics That Matter

- Open rate: 25%+ is good, 35%+ is great
- Click rate: 3%+ is solid
- Reply rate: Track this for engagement
- Unsubscribe rate: Under 0.5% is healthy
